## Target Regions & Key Areas

### Northern Portugal - Prime Zones

**Porto District (Best Overall)**

- **Specific towns**: Amarante, Penafiel, Marco de Canaveses
- **Why**: 30-45 mins from Porto airport, beautiful Douro valley, good healthcare access
- **Property budget**: €300k-600k for substantial properties with land

**Minho Province (Closest to Spain)**

- **Specific towns**: Ponte de Lima, Viana do Castelo, Barcelos
- **Why**: Cooler climate, stunning countryside, lower prices
- **Property budget**: €200k-500k

**Douro Valley (Wine Country)**

- **Specific towns**: Lamego, Peso da Régua, Vila Real
- **Why**: World Heritage area, tourism infrastructure, beautiful setting
- **Property budget**: €250k-500k

### Southwest France - Prime Zones

**Lot Department (Best Value)**

- **Specific towns**: Cahors, Figeac, Gourdon
- **Why**: Affordable, beautiful countryside, established expat community
- **Property budget**: €300k-700k for properties with significant land

**Languedoc-Roussillon (Best Climate)**

- **Specific towns**: Pézenas, Clermont-l'Hérault, Lodève (inland from Montpellier)
- **Why**: Great climate, reasonable prices inland
- **Property budget**: €400k-800k

**Dordogne (Most English-Friendly)**

- **Specific towns**: Sarlat, Bergerac, Périgueux area
- **Why**: Large expat community, established support networks
- **Property budget**: €350k-700k

## Essential Search Criteria for Gîte Properties

### Must-Haves

- **Land size**: Minimum 2,000m² (for privacy and pool)
- **Bedrooms**: 4-6 (3 for you, 1-3 for guests)
- **Water source**: Mains water or excellent well
- **Electrical capacity**: 30+ amps (for pool equipment, heat pumps)
- **Road access**: Year-round vehicle access
- **Planning permission**: Ability to add pool and modify for business use

### Highly Desirable

- **Existing pool** (saves €30k-50k investment)
- **Outbuildings** (storage, pump house, workshop)
- **Southern exposure** (solar potential, warmth)
- **Views** (marketing appeal for guests)

### Red Flags to Avoid

- **Flood zones** (check government flood maps)
- **Historic protection zones** (renovation restrictions)
- **Active farms nearby** (noise, smells, early morning activity)
- **Major roads nearby** (noise, safety concerns)
- **Poor mobile/internet signal** (essential for bookings)

## Research Tools & Resources

### Property Research

- **Google Earth** - Check surroundings, privacy, access roads
- **Government cadastral maps** - Verify boundaries, neighbors
- **Local mairies (town halls)** - Planning permissions, regulations
- **Tourism office websites** - Local attractions, seasonal patterns

### Practical Planning

- **Drive time to airports** - Porto (Portugal), Toulouse/Montpellier (France)
- **Hospital locations** - Especially gastroenterology services
- **Internet speed maps** - Critical for online bookings
- **Local business registers** - Competition, regulations

## Budget Planning Beyond Purchase Price

### Portugal Additional Costs

- **Purchase tax (IMT)**: 0-8% depending on price
- **Registration fees**: ~€500-1,500
- **Legal fees**: €1,500-3,000
- **Survey**: €500-1,000

### France Additional Costs

- **Notaire fees**: 7-8% of purchase price
- **Survey**: €1,000-2,000
- **Legal fees**: €2,000-4,000
- **Property tax**: Varies significantly by commune

### Renovation/Setup Costs (Both Countries)

- **Pool installation**: €25k-50k if needed
- **Solar panels**: €15k-25k (with government rebates)
- **Heat pump system**: €10k-20k
- **Business setup/licensing**: €2k-5k

## Warning Signs in Property Listings

### Immediate Red Flags

- **"Needs some work"** often means major structural issues
- **No internal photos** usually indicates serious problems
- **Priced significantly below comparable properties** - investigate why
- **Multiple price reductions** - property may have hidden issues
- **"Ideal for renovation"** - factor in 50-100% more costs than estimated

### Questions to Ask Every Agent

1. Why is the property being sold?
2. How long has it been on the market?
3. What are the annual property taxes?
4. Are there any planning restrictions?
5. What's the internet speed/mobile coverage?
6. How far to nearest gastroenterology services?
