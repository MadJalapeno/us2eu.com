## Critical Tax Planning Questions

### Roth Conversion Strategy

1. **Pre-Move Conversions**: Should we do large Roth conversions in our final US tax years? What's the optimal amount per year?
    
2. **Post-Move Conversions**: Can we continue Roth conversions after moving abroad using the Foreign Earned Income Exclusion? How does gîte business income factor in?
    
3. **Timing Strategy**: What's the ideal sequence - convert first then move, or move first then convert?
    
4. **Tax Bracket Management**: How do we optimize conversions across multiple years to minimize overall tax burden?
    

### Country-Specific Retirement Account Treatment

5. **Portugal Taxation**: How will Portugal tax our 401(k) and Roth IRA distributions? Does the new NHR 2.0 (IFICI) program affect this?
    
6. **France vs Portugal**: Which country offers better treatment of US retirement accounts?
    
7. **Double Taxation**: How do tax treaties protect us from being double-taxed on retirement income?
    
8. **Social Security**: How will each country tax our future Social Security benefits?
    

### Business Income Considerations

## Red Flags to Watch For

- Advisors who don't understand foreign tax implications
- Anyone promising "tax-free" solutions that sound too good to be true
- Firms that don't mention FBAR, FATCA, or other expat compliance requirements
- Advisors who can't explain the specific tax treaties between US and your target country
