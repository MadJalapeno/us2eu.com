## User Question 15

> If we forget about wanting to do the gite what would you suggest (remembering all of the previous content of this chat)?

### Claudes Response

Removing the gîte from the equation actually simplifies things significantly and opens up more options! Let me reassess your priorities:

**Your Core Needs:**

- Healthcare for Crohn's disease (Stelara access)
- Progressive political environment
- Good climate
- Reasonable cost of living
- Easy UK access
- Quality of life

**Revised Recommendations:**

## **1. Northern Portugal (Still #1 Choice)**

**Why it's even better without the gîte business:**

- **Much wider location options** - you're not limited to rural properties with land
- **Consider coastal towns** like Viana do Castelo, Póvoa de Varzim, or even Porto suburbs
- **Apartment living possible** - less maintenance, better urban amenities
- **Your $800k goes even further** - excellent apartments €200k-400k, houses €300k-500k
- **D7 visa even easier** without business complications

**Best locations now:**

- **Porto area**: Urban amenities, excellent healthcare, culture, airport access
- **Braga**: University town, younger vibe, excellent value
- **Coastal towns**: Great climate, lower prices than France

## **2. Southern Spain (Now Much More Viable)**

**Why it jumps up the list:**

- **Excellent healthcare** - covers Stelara, world-class facilities
- **Perfect climate** for your lifestyle
- **Much cheaper than France**
- **Easy UK access** - direct flights from multiple cities
- **No business visa complications**

**Best regions:**

- **Valencia area**: Perfect climate, progressive, reasonable prices, excellent healthcare
- **Andalusia inland**: Granada, Córdoba areas - cultural richness, affordability
- **Catalonia**: Barcelona region - very progressive, excellent amenities

## **3. Southern France (Now More Accessible)**

**Without the business element:**

- **Visitor visa much simpler** than business visa
- **Wider location options** - not limited to rural properties
- **Consider smaller cities** like Montpellier, Nîmes, Perpignan
- **Apartment living possible** - easier maintenance

## **My Updated Recommendation: Portugal D7**

**Specifically, consider Porto or Braga:**

- **Urban lifestyle** but with easy countryside access
- **Excellent healthcare infrastructure**
- **Vibrant expat communities**
- **Great restaurants, culture, nightlife**
- **Easy travel throughout Europe**
- **Your money goes furthest here**

**Lifestyle Benefits:**

- **No property maintenance headaches** of running a business
- **Freedom to travel** without worrying about bookings
- **Easier integration** in urban communities
- **Less isolation** than rural gîte life
- **Better healthcare access** in cities

**Your $800k could buy:**

- **Luxury Porto apartment** (€300k) + €500k invested for income
- **Beautiful house in Braga** (€400k) + €400k invested
- **Coastal property** + significant remaining funds

Would urban living in Porto or a university town like Braga interest you? Or are you still drawn to rural life, just without the business complications?
